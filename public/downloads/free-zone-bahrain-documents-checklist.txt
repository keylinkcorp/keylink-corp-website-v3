Free Zone in Bahrain — Documents Checklist (Keylink Corp)

Use this checklist as a starting point. The exact requirements vary by activity, shareholders, and whether a corporate shareholder is involved.

1) Shareholder / Owner documents
- Passport copy (all shareholders)
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement, typically recent)
- Contact details (email + phone)

2) Company details
- Proposed company name options
- Business activity description (what you do, how you earn revenue)
- Shareholding structure and % split

3) If a corporate shareholder is involved
- Certificate of incorporation
- Memorandum / Articles of Association
- Board resolution authorizing the Bahrain company setup
- UBO (ultimate beneficial owner) details

4) Address / premises
- Zone preference (BLZ / BIIP / BIW / Sitra)
- Space requirement (sqm) and intended use (office / warehouse / light manufacturing)
- Draft lease / address confirmation (as required)

5) Optional (activity-dependent)
- Business plan
- Product lists / HS codes (for trade / import-export)
- Sector approvals and supporting certificates
